Partnering with Meteor Camp NYC

We have defined four (4) partner tiers to allow for broad participation. From our Platinum level ($5,000) all the way down to our Community tier ($500), we've striven to minimize the cost for supporters so that all can find a way to participate. Two (2) special add-on levels allow partners to target specific spending, with the Keynote add-on ($2,500) designed to defray the international travel, lodging and food costs for our keynote speaker.
Founding partners will enjoy discounts on future annual Meteor Camp NYC events. If you want to know more, contact us using the link below.
